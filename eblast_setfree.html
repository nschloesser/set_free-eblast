By far the greatest “encumbrance” (Hebrews 12:2) to the church and believers today is pervasive Internet pornography.

The Barna Group declared, “Christian men are watching pornography at work at the same rate as the national average.” In fact, another survey showed that 3 out of 5 Christian men and 1 out of 7 Christian women watch porn at least once a month.

What is your church doing about this issue?

Many churches don’t know how to handle porn use by their members. A recent poll by Covenant Eyes revealed that there are many barriers preventing churches from helping people find healing and recovery against this secret sin. Challenges range from addressing sexual sin to a diverse congregation (including kids) to not knowing how to make their church feel like a safe place for strugglers to open up and ask for help.
